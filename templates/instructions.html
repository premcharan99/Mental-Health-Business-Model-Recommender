Instructions

Use the dashboard to input details about your mental health service. The system will predict the top business models suitable for your setup.

Attributes

  • Service Type: Type of service (e.g., Teletherapy, Outpatient).
  • Ownership: Ownership model (e.g., Private For-Profit, Nonprofit).
  • Budget: Budget in USD (5,000 to 500,000).
  • Target Age Group: Primary audience (e.g., Young Adults, Seniors).
  • Location: Service location (e.g., Urban Global, Rural Local).
  • Market Demand: Demand level (Low, Medium, High).
  • Delivery Mode: Service delivery (Online, In-Person, Hybrid).
  • Payment Methods: Payment options (e.g., Cash, Insurance).
  • Accessibility Goal: Accessibility priority (Low, Medium, High).

After submitting, view predictions and visualizations on the dashboard. Explore the dataset and additional visualizations via the navigation menu.
